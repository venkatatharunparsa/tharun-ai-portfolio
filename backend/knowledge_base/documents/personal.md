## On Startups and Products

My interest in entrepreneurship came before my interest in AI. I got into technology because it was the fastest path to building products that matter.

I believe the best products come from personal frustration. Career Guide came from spending too many hours searching for internships. NINA came from watching people struggle with bad interfaces. TaxSetu came from a real fine that hit someone we know.

Startups fail when founders stop too soon, skip problem validation, or build what they assume users need instead of what users actually need. I learned this early.
